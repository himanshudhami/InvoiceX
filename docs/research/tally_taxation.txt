GST & Compliance
----------------

![Image 113: Smart financial statements for non-corporates ](https://resources.tallysolutions.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Manufacturing%E2%80%A8journal-2.svg)

Smart financial statements for non-corporates

TallyPrime’s Excel-based template is designed Chartered Accountants, tax practitioners and businesses to simplify and speed up financial reporting for non-corporate. Built as per ICAI's latest guidelines, it enables easy import of trial balance/balance sheet/ profit & loss a/c from TallyPrime and auto-captures the details to financial statement template, saving hours of manual effort. [Enable Add-On ![Image 114: arrow-up-right-sharp](https://resources.tallysolutions.com/wp-content/themes/tally/assets/images/arrow-up-right-sharp.svg)](http://tallysolutions.com/addon-financial-statements-nce-tallyprime/)

![Image 115: Invoice Management System (IMS)](https://resources.tallysolutions.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/Multiple-billing-1.svg)

Invoice Management System (IMS)

TallyPrime’s connected IMS solution enables you to seamlessly download, reconcile, and take actions—Accept, Reject, or Pending on e-invoices—directly within TallyPrime, keeping your books and IMS dashboard in sync. What’s more?
